Introductory Rites — Gloria

44 – AS GENTLE AS SILENCE (p. 11)
Oh, the love of my Lord is the
essence,
Of all that I love here on earth,
All the beauty I see he has given to
me,
And his giving is gentle as silence.
Every day, every hour, every
moment,
Has been blessed by the strength
of his love,
At the turn of each tide, he is there
at my side,
and his touch is as gentle as
silence.
There’ve been times when I’ve
turned from his presence,
And I've walked other paths, other
ways,
But I’ve called on his name.
In the dark of my shame,
And his mercy was gentle as
silence.
