Important information for members of the group water scheme

Billing The Scheme operates an annual billing system. Your service meter is read each year in December. Invoices are issued in January.Usage is calculated by deducting previous reading from current reading.If you have more than one meter installed the same procedure as above applies for all meters as each meter has a separate identification number. If you qualify for a domestic allowance, the current domestic allowance is applied to each invoice. (Current domestic allowance is 110 cubic meters per year).
